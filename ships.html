Ships

Every entry in data/configs/ships/ plus every item with type SHIP. The game has 5 ship classes and 4 factions; components (chassis / cargo / shield / engine) are class-locked, see the box below.

Spotting your ships: the Player badge in the Pilot column flags every hull you can actually fly — derived from the model's asset path (data/models/.../player*/). Pirates / Police / NPC variants get their own badges. The "scout" you start with is Micro Recon (T1 Corvette, base price 6000). Other early hulls: Cube Basic, Ultraviolet Intruder. Higher-tier player ships (Argonaut, Excalibur, Leviathan, Neutron) sit at the bottom of the Generic / Ancients buckets.
Can I apply scout-ship parts to a newer ship? No. Ship components in data/configs/recipes/V_*.cfg are split into four parallel ladders — V_CORVETTE_*, V_FRIGATE_*, V_DREADNOUGHT_*, V_GALLEON_* — each with its own MK1 → MK5 progression. There is no recipe in the data that converts a Corvette part to a Frigate part. Within a class you upgrade by crafting MKn+1 components; across classes you scrap your old setup or sell the old ship outright.
