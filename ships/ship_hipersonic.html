Buying / upgrading

This is a Corvette-class ship. To upgrade it, craft components from the V_CORVETTE_* recipe family (MK1 → MK5):

  • Chassis — hull integrity; MK5 variants exist as named alts (e.g. Excalibur, Red Fury for Corvettes).
  • Cargo — inventory capacity in the ship's hold.
  • Shield — energy shield buffer before hull damage.
  • Engine (SPACE_ENGINE) — sublight / warp speed.

You cannot transplant a Corvette component into a Frigate (or vice versa) — see the note on the Ships overview.
