Outpost perks

"Perks" in Cubic Odyssey are outpost-bound — they live in data/configs/outpostperks/ as OutpostPerkCfg blocks and they apply to the outpost you've activated them at, not your player character globally. The data ships 3 perks, with id slot 2 unfilled (likely planned but cut, or reserved for future content).

There is no separate "character perk" system in the configs — the player's progression goes through the nine skills (Mining, Trading, etc., one max_level_* achievement each), and these three outpost perks. If you've seen a perk UI in-game that doesn't match what's listed here, let us know — the configs are sparse and the engine may surface effects we can't see from data alone.

Where you spend them (the OUTPOST_LEADER NPC)

Perks are managed by the Outpost Leader. From data/configs/characters/npc_outpost_leader.cfg:

CharacterCfg
{
    interaction          OUTPOST_LEADER
    defaultTeam          PLAYER
    ai_template          "outpost_sniper"
    weapon               "wep.rifle.sma15e.1"
    life                 1000
    shieldCapacity       (none — they're regular HP, but plot-armoured at 1000)
}

Walk up to the leader at any outpost you've liberated or built; the OUTPOST_LEADER interaction opens the perk panel. The exact cost / unlock condition (qubits, outpost level, materials) isn't exposed in the configs — it's calculated by the engine — but the panel itself is where you spend.

Which perk to take first

  1. Trade Discount first. It's the only perk with a hard-numeric value in the configs (2.0% per level), and it compounds with the Trading skill discount — every level of every perk you stack here pays back across every future merchant transaction.
  2. Processing Speed second, if you smelt or refine in bulk. The data doesn't quote the multiplier, but anecdotally it shaves enough off cook times to matter when you're processing tier-3 / tier-4 ore stacks.
  3. HP Regen last. You have a fragile 60-HP player, but Suits + the Doctor NPC + consumables already cover most healing needs. The perk only matters in long expeditions where you're nowhere near an outpost — by which point you're already past the early game.

How perks stack with everything else

  • Per outpost, not per player. Each outpost has its own perk slots. Taking Trade Discount at Outpost A doesn't give you the discount at Outpost B — you have to take it there too.
  • Trade Discount stacks multiplicatively with the Trading skill. 2% perk × Trading-skill discount = compounded reduction at checkout.
  • Processing Speed applies to that outpost's furnaces and refineries. Carry-portable cookware (if any) isn't affected.
  • HP Regen presumably applies only inside the outpost radius. The engine almost certainly treats the perk like an aura; we don't have the exact radius in the configs.

What the configs don't tell us

  • The exact effect of HP Regen (HP per second?) and Processing Speed (× multiplier?) — only their existence and ids are in the files.
  • The cost to unlock each level (qubits, materials, outpost XP).
  • The level cap. The _1 suffix on every filename suggests the design supports tiers (HP_REGEN_2, HP_REGEN_3, …), but no level-2 files exist in the data we have. The engine likely scales m_value by an internal level counter rather than spawning more cfg files.
  • What slot id 2 was meant to be. If you remember a fourth perk from a patch note or update, let us know — we'd happily fill it in here.
