Leveling Mining

The Mining skill is one of nine player skills in Cubic Odyssey (confirmed by data/configs/achievements/max_level_mining.cfg). It governs how quickly you break voxel-tier ores and how much ore you recover per swing.

How XP is earned

Mining XP comes from breaking ore voxels — every successful mining hit that lands on a block in category E_VCAT_ORES (the 26 ores listed on the Ores page) credits Mining experience. Non-ore voxels (Stone, Limestone, Cliff, Lava etc.) do not. Higher-tier ores award more XP per block.

Step 1 — Always carry the best mining laser you can use

There are 8 mining lasers in the game. Each has a voxel_tier stat that caps the ore tier it can break:

Step 2 — Match ore tier to your skill, not to your laser

Your laser determines which ores you can mine; your skill level (combined with the laser's voxel_damage and fireRateRPM) determines how fast. The most efficient grind is mining a tier you can break in under 2 seconds per block.

Step 3 — Backpack tier multiplies efficiency

Each tier of backpack increases inventory capacity. Running back to a refinery every few veins wastes mining time. Backpack 5 is the realistic late-game target — see the Motherboards guide for the materials it eats.

Step 4 — Pick the right planet type

Ore concentration varies wildly between planet types. Use the ores table to filter by tier, then click into an ore to see its location table. Quick highlights:

  • Earth-like worlds (Earth, Bardwell I–III) host every tier-1 and tier-2 ore in good density. Best starter loop.
  • Crystalline worlds are the gem (T5) hotspot.
  • Scorched worlds stack rare-matter ores (T6) and Glowing variants.
  • Barren worlds are good for T3–T4 (Magnetite, Cobalt) without the danger of scorched / xeno biomes.

Tips that compound

  • Mine in vertical shafts. Frequency (m_frequency) is the same across all Y-layers for most ores, but vein extent (m_extent) is highest in the deep layers. Drop a shaft to bedrock and mine outward.
  • Don't sell ore — smelt it. The ingot form is worth more than the raw ore and is what crafting wants anyway.
  • Glowing ores aren't just shinier — they're a separate tier-7 drop. They smelt into Glowing Ingots used in late-game crafting.
  • Mining skill XP rewards are global — there's no per-planet cap. Pick a deep iron vein on Earth and grind cleanly.
