Vendor-only items

These are items the player cannot craft or loot — they show up in merchant inventories instead. The list is computed by cross-referencing every item against:

  • data/configs/recipes/ — no recipe produces this item
  • data/configs/randomsets/ — not in any creature / bunker / pirate drop table
  • data/configs/voxels/ — not a mineable ore drop

If all three sources miss, the item is sourced from merchants. 292 items match. Sorted by base_demand ascending — the most-supplied (vendor-stocked) items come first.

Why this matters: the parts on this list gate most of your tier-3 / tier-4 crafting. Motherboards, mirrors, capacitors, transistors, ferroglass lenses, wiring — every advanced recipe asks for them, and the only way to stockpile is to buy in bulk. Roll the Trade Discount outpost perk first.
