Vendor-only items

These are items the player cannot craft or loot — they show up in merchant inventories instead. The list is computed by cross-referencing every item against four signals:

  • data/configs/recipes/ — no recipe produces this item
  • data/configs/randomsets/ — not in any creature / bunker / pirate drop table
  • data/configs/voxels/ — not a mineable ore drop
  • base_demand < 0 — the item's own config marks vendors as net suppliers (without this check the list fills up with truly-unobtainable saplings, fruits, and enemy-only weapons that simply happen to fail the first three tests)

69 items match. Sorted by base_demand ascending — the most-supplied (vendor-stocked) items come first.

Why this matters: the four boards on this list — Circuit Board, Logic Board, Motherboard, Quantum Processor — gate practically every advanced craft, and the rest is suit / jetpack / mining / weapon MODs you can't make yourself. Vendors are the only source. Roll the Trade Discount outpost perk first.
