Leveling Mining

The Mining skill is one of nine player skills in Cubic Odyssey (confirmed by data/configs/achievements/max_level_mining.cfg). It governs how quickly you break voxel-tier ores and how much ore you recover per swing.

Set your level to highlight the right stage below.

What to do at your level

Beginning level 1–9
Tier-1 ores with the starter laser. Build your first stockpile.
  • Equip Mining Laser 0 (voxel_tier 1). It's the only thing you have access to and it breaks the four tier-1 ores: Iron, Coal, Aluminum, Copper.
  • Stay on an Earth-like world (Earth or Bardwell I–III). They host every tier-1 ore in dense layers.
  • Smelt everything you mine. Don't sell raw ore — ingot value is higher and crafting wants ingots.
  • Save up for the Mining Laser 1 + Backpack 2 craft as soon as Crafting allows.
Mid game level 10–19
Tier-2/3 ores. Gold + Silver are the value-per-swing sweet spot.
  • Switch to Mining Laser 2 when available (voxel_tier 3). It one-shots tier-1 and breaks tier-2/3 cleanly.
  • Target tier-2 ores: Gold, Silver, Silicon, Tin. The Earth-like distribution still works — they spawn alongside the tier-1s.
  • Start dipping into tier-3: Titanium, Magnetite, Sulfur, Quartz.
  • Drop vertical shafts. Frequency stays roughly even per layer, but the deep layers are where you'll find tier-3 veins.
  • Goal: Mining Laser 3 + Backpack 3 by level 20.
Advanced level 20–29
Tier-4 ores carry real XP weight; Crystalline worlds open up for gems.
  • Run Mining Laser 3 → 4 (voxel_tier 4 → 5). Laser 4 one-shots most tier-3, makes tier-4 viable, and unlocks gems.
  • Tier-4 ore loop: Cobalt, Uranium, Polonium, Radium. Best density on Barren worlds.
  • Switch planets for gems: Crystalline worlds host Diamond, Ruby, Emerald, Sapphire.
  • Pick up the Motherboards guide — you'll soon want Backpack 5, Scanner 4, and the Mining Laser 4 craft, all of which eat them.
Late game level 30–39
Gem farming on Crystalline; chase the tier-6 Matter ores when you can.
  • Mining Laser 5 (voxel_tier 6) lets you crack tier-6 Matter ores. If you're still on Laser 4, gems are your ceiling for now.
  • Farm gems on Crystalline worlds; sell the surplus through your Trading grind.
  • Begin scouting Scorched worlds for tier-6 Matter (Antimatter, Dark Matter, etc.). Bring a Scanner.
  • Backpack 5 should be done by now; if not, route motherboards toward it first.
Endgame level 40+
Glowing ores and Higgs Bosson; specific biomes only.
  • Mining Laser 6 or 7 (voxel_tier 7). Laser 7 has higher DPS but doesn't unlock anything Laser 6 can't already break.
  • Hunt Glowing variants (Glowing Iron, Glowing Cobalt, Glowing Magnetite, …). Smelt into Glowing Ingots for endgame craft inputs.
  • Higgs Bosson is the only tier-7 non-glowing voxel — extremely rare; bring scanners.
  • Push toward the Mining skill max achievement (ACH_MAX_LEVEL_MINING).

How XP is earned

Mining XP comes from breaking ore voxels — every successful mining hit that lands on a block in category E_VCAT_ORES (the 26 ores listed on the Ores page) credits Mining experience. Non-ore voxels (Stone, Limestone, Cliff, Lava etc.) do not. Higher-tier ores award more XP per block.

Universal tips

  • Smelt before selling. Ingots are worth more than raw ore.
  • Mine in vertical shafts. Drop to bedrock, mine outward; the deep layers carry the highest m_extent veins.
  • Always carry the highest-tier laser you have. The per-swing damage on a Laser 5 is bigger than the equivalent rank on Laser 0, so even on tier-1 ores you'll mine faster.
  • Glowing variants are separate items, not skins. They smelt into Glowing Ingots used for endgame crafting and are gated tier-7.
