What it's used for

Glowing ores aren't used directly in crafting — they're an intermediate. The flow is always: mine → smelt → craft.

  1. Mine the voxel with a tier-7 mining laser (Mining Laser 6 or 7). Lower-tier lasers do nothing to it.
  2. Smelt in a furnace (20s, 3 fuel) into Glowing Cobalt Ingot.
  3. Craft: the ingot is a crafting input for 3 recipes across high-tier weapons, ship parts, and gear.

Tip: there's a craftable Glowing Ore Extractor deployable (tier 5, requires CRAFTING 16) that processes glowing ores in bulk. It costs 10 Gem Plates, 5 Super Conductors, plus 8 each of Antimatter, Dark Matter, and Gamma Matter Cells — so don't try to craft it before you've mined a healthy stockpile of tier-6 Matter ores.

Selling angle: the raw ore sells for 260 qubits each with positive vendor demand (base_demand 0.5). Smelting first roughly halves the inventory volume and lifts the per-unit value — sell ingots, not raw ore.
